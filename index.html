How To Play

Welcome to Guess The Dungeon!

You and your enemy each have hidden attack squares.

Click tiles to uncover them:

  • 🟩 Green tiles — Your attacks that do 20 damage the enemy.
  • 🟥 Red tiles — Enemy attacks that do 20 damage you.
  • ⬛ Gray tiles — Blank spaces.

Defeat enemies to progress through levels.

Every 3rd level, you’ll gain a helpful item.

Collect items to become stronger and reach the boss on every 10th level!
