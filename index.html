Guess The Dungeon

How To Play

Welcome to Guess The Dungeon! — a mix of luck, strategy, and risk-taking.

🎯 Your Goal

Each level, you and an enemy take turns revealing tiles on the grid. Some tiles are safe, while others trigger attacks!

  • 🟩 Green tiles — Your attacks! Deal damage to the enemy.
  • 🟥 Red tiles — Enemy attacks! You take damage.
  • ⬛ Blank tiles — Safe. Nothing happens.

⚔️ Combos

Each time you land a hit without missing, your damage increases slightly. This is called your combo multiplier.

  • Every successful hit raises your combo (up to 2× damage max).
  • Missing a tile or getting hit resets your combo back to 1×.
  • Watch for the glowing “Combo!” popup when you strike with boosted power.

🧱 Levels and Difficulty

As you climb through the dungeon, enemies get stronger:

  • Enemies gain more health and deal more damage every few levels.
  • Every 10th level is a Boss Battle — tougher enemies with extra health and higher attack power!
  • After a boss, enemies become stronger in future fights.

💎 Items and the Shop

Every 3 levels, you visit a shop and can pick one item to keep for the rest of your run.

  • Each item gives you a special bonus, such as more health, more attacks, healing, or defense.
  • You can only choose one item per shop visit — choose wisely!

Item Rarities

Items come in different rarities, which affect their power and how often you find them:

  • 🟩 Common — 55% chance, simple small boosts.
  • 🟦 Rare — 25% chance, better effects.
  • 🟪 Epic — 15% chance, powerful bonuses.
  • 🟨 Legendary — 5% chance, the best and rarest items!

❤️ Health and Healing

  • Your health is shown at the top of the screen in green.
  • The enemy’s health is red.
  • Some items heal you after attacking or between levels.
  • If your health reaches zero, you lose — unless you have an item that revives you!

🔁 Revives

Special items like the Life Amulet or Phoenix Heart can bring you back from defeat:

  • Each revive item works only once.
  • When it activates, you’ll return to life with a portion (or all) of your health restored.

🏆 Progression

  • Each level, you’ll face a new challenge with stronger enemies.
  • Win to advance and earn new items.
  • Lose, and your run restarts from Level 1 — but you’ll be better prepared next time!

⏸️ Tips

  • Use the Pause Button (⏸️) to take a break during battle.
  • Hover over your Health Bars to see exact HP numbers.
  • Open your Inventory (🎒) to view all collected items during your run.

Good luck, adventurer. The dungeon awaits!
